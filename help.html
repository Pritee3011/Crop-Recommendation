How Can We Help You Grow?

Here is a guide to navigating the Farm-Forward dashboard and utilizing its tools.

Crop Info

This section provides detailed agronomic data for every crop in our database, including optimal temperature, soil type, and rainfall requirements.

Key Feature: Search Bar

Use the Search bar at the top of the Crop Info page to quickly find any crop. Type its name (e.g., Apple or PigeonPeas) and press Enter. The page scrolls directly to the correct card and highlights it.

Recommendations

The Recommendations feature (coming soon!) will utilize your region's weather and soil data to suggest the best crops for maximizing yield and profitability.

How it Works:
  • Input your current location details (e.g., pH level, temperature range).
  • The system runs an analysis against crop requirements.
  • It presents a ranked list of suggested crops.

Get Support

If you encounter an issue or have a question about crop science or the app itself, we're here to help!

Contact Us:
